Libre Docs - Be free

Libre Docs is different from most other websites. It only admits unhosted users. This means that:

  • It does not store any of the documents you edit on its servers.
  • It also does not track you.
  • It does not even remember you in any way at all [except in the browserid-verifier cache, but we're working on that].
All the Libre Docs applications run in your browser, under your control. For this to work, you have to sync and publish your documents to your own remote storage.
