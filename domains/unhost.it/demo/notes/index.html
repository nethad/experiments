RAM is simple TODO list manager. Data are stored in your browser using remoteStorage.

To add task click on empty cell, enter text and hit "enter" (submit key / outer area). In open task you can change color of cell or delete note.

To add/delete cell click on last cell marked with plus/minus icon.

Click logo to access help section.
